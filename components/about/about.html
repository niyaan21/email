The Problem

For close to 40 years, we have used different ways to send emails for recreation and work. However, as email communication evolved, the sheer volume of daily emails has become overwhelming. This challenge necessitates a better way to manage emails efficiently, and that’s why we created Fosffiliate.

The Solution

Fosffiliate is a powerful email summarizer that evaluates the importance of your emails. It condenses long, complex messages into a few concise bullet points, making them easier to read without losing their key information.

About

Our Mission:

Fosffiliate has one simple goal, which is to make reading emails and responding to them faster and simpler for businesses. Emails are a crucial mode of communication, but sorting and organizing them into tags to stay organized takes time. Fosffiliate aims to streamline this process, making it invaluable for all types of businesses across almost every niche.

Outreach is a vital part of any business, and many entrepreneurs spend up to 80% of their time managing it. By providing summaries of all emails and categorizing them into different tags, Fosffiliate reduces the time it takes to read and respond to messages. Additionally, you can still access all of your previous features from Gmail, Mail, Outlook, and other services.

In conclusion, Fosffiliate is a summarizing tool that can propel your business into the future of email management, enhancing productivity by letting you focus on more pressing matters instead of wasting time reading lengthy and often irrelevant emails.

Over 90% of emails are unnecessarily long, with the important information often condensed into a couple of sentences. Businesses receive huge volumes of emails, and going through them daily takes a lot of time. By summarizing these emails, Fosffiliate helps businesses save time and focus on what matters most.
