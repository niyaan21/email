Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?
Our services include summarizing emails, creating a new and refreshing interface for your mails, and categorizing your emails from most important to least important in an organized and systematic fashion.
What devices can I use Fosffiliate on?
You can use Fosffiliate on any tablet, phone, or laptop that has access to the Play Store or App Store. You can also download it from our website after payment.
Who does Fosffiliate benefit?
Everyone. Anyone who has any type of email account will definitely be able to work more efficiently and save time throughout the day using our service.
Is Fosffiliate Free?
No, Fosffiliate is not free. However, we have a demo on our website which can be used to experience the user interface and check out how we summarize emails.
Does Fosffiliate run on iOS machines?
Yes, it does. It can either be downloaded from the App Store or from our website as well.
What type of emails will Fosffiliate be able to summarize?
Fosffiliate has the ability to summarize personal emails, professional emails, long threads of emails, newsletters, etc.
What will be the format of the summaries?
Fosffiliate will by default format the summaries in bullet points, however, you can change the format to paragraphs as well through your settings icon.
How will we input emails?
Since Fosffiliate uses direct email integration through Gmail, there is no need to input your emails manually.
How will my data be managed?
In order to know about Fosffiliate's privacy policy, click here.
How accurate are the email summaries?
Our AI-powered summarization technology is highly accurate, capturing the key points of emails with over 95% accuracy. However, for critical emails, we always recommend reviewing the full content.
Can Fosffiliate integrate with my existing email client?
Yes, Fosffiliate can integrate with popular email clients like Gmail, Outlook, and Apple Mail. We're constantly working on expanding our integration capabilities.
What pricing plans do you offer?
We offer flexible pricing plans to suit different needs: Basic, Pro, and Enterprise. Each plan offers different features and email volume capabilities. Please visit our Pricing page for detailed information.
Is there a limit to how many emails Fosffiliate can summarize?
The email limit depends on your chosen plan. Our Basic plan covers up to 1000 emails per month, while our Enterprise plan offers unlimited email summarization.
How does Fosffiliate handle attachments?
Fosffiliate notes the presence of attachments in its summaries but does not summarize the content of attachments. The original attachments remain accessible in your email client.
Can I customize the level of detail in the summaries?
Yes, you can adjust the summary length in your settings. Choose from brief, standard, or detailed summaries to suit your preferences.
Does Fosffiliate work with emails in languages other than English?
Currently, Fosffiliate supports English, Spanish, French, and German. We're working on adding support for more languages in the near future.
How secure is my email data with Fosffiliate?
We take data security very seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not store the full content of your emails, only the generated summaries. For more details, please review our security policy.
Is there a mobile app for Fosffiliate?
Yes, we offer mobile apps for both iOS and Android devices. You can download them from the App Store or Google Play Store respectively.
What kind of customer support do you offer?
We offer 24/7 email support for all users. Our Pro and Enterprise plans also include priority support with faster response times and access to phone support.
